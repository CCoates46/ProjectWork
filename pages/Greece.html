Top three things to do in Santorini, Greece

Horse-riding

An alternative way to experience Santorini is by horse riding. If you love horses then you will definitely like this activity that will make you feel close to nature. There is a variety of tours you can follow and do horse riding either on the beach or in the main land. The experience is very romantic and special. You can ask our concierge team for the available excursions.

Visit a winery

Santorinian wines are world famous and once visiting the island you should try as many varieties of local wines as you can. The volcanic soil of the island adds up to the unique flavors and wine is one of the most important local products for the residents. Visit one of the many wineries that operate on the island and have a wine tasting experience. Most vineyards also offer food pairing advices to complete your taste journey. You can also learn anything you like about the wines and their production procedure by asking the expert professionals that will be there to guide and assist you.
